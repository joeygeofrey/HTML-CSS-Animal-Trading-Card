African Penguin

African penguins have a number of dot-like markings flecked across their white chests.

  • Latin Name: Spheniscus demersus
  • Weight: 4.4-11 lbs
  • Size: 2.2-2.3 feet
  • Lifespan: 10-15 years

African penguins make loud braying sounds, which has given them an alternative name ‘Jackass penguin’.

Siberian Tiger

Tigers are the largest of all wild cats and are renowned for their power and strength.

  • Latin Name: Panthera tigris altaica
  • Weight: 400-700 lbs
  • Size: 9-12 feet
  • Lifespan: 16-18 years

Siberian tigers differ from other tigers having fewer, paler stripes, and they also have manes - no two have exactly the same stripes.

Great Hammerhead

Their wide-set eyes give them a better visual range than most other sharks.

  • Latin Name: Sphyrna mokarran
  • Weight: 600-1000 lbs
  • Size: 13-20 feet
  • Lifespan: 20-30 years

Great hammerheads have very impressive triangular, serrated teeth—like the edge of a saw's blade.
